## Overview

This PhD seminar course (FTN0509) covers foundational ideas spanning learning and control theory. The course runs across two semesters with nine lectures followed by student presentations.

## Topics

- Entropy and information
- Dynamical systems and state-space representations
- Frequency domain methods
- Maximum likelihood and risk minimisation
- Stochastic approximation and adaptation
- Principle of optimality
- Convex optimization

## Format

Lecture-based sessions with paired discussion forums, culminating in final student presentations.
